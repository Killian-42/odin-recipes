Ribs

Description

This little paragraph should describe what ribs are and why you should make them. Ribs are the ribs of an animal, commonly pigs. We kill them mercilessly in abattoirs, cut them into delicious little pieces, package them all over the world and eat them. The ribs are some of the best bits.

Ribs are tender, tasty and give you an excuse to eat like a caveman in decent company. You eat it with your hands, get sauce all over your beard and nobody thinks less of you, it's amazing.

Ingredients

These are the things you will need for this recipe

  • Raw ribs
  • Sauce of your choosing
  • Heat
  • Patience

Steps

Steps

  1. Slather the ribs in the sauce of your choosing
  2. Put them in the oven
  3. Wait
  4. Enjoy

Congratulations!! You have made ribs, well done you!
